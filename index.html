My Story

My journey, my experience, and the reason I created Negin Nail Academy.

My journey in the nail industry started with a true passion for beauty, but very quickly it became something deeper. I wanted to master structure, retention, shaping, and high-end salon techniques that create flawless, long-lasting results.

Through my work, I saw that many nail techs struggle with lifting, shaping, speed, and confidence with clients, not because they are not talented, but because they were never taught the right system in the right way.

That is why I created Negin Nail Academy. Inside my courses, I share the exact step-by-step methods, salon-tested techniques, and real business knowledge that helped me build a loyal clientele, create premium services, generate consistent income, and work with confidence behind the table.

This is not just nail training. It is a complete system for becoming a confident, skilled, and successful nail technician.
